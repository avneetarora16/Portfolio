Subject: Usability and Human Interaction
Task: Problem solving what???
Duration: 12 weeks
Project Overview
This was our project for the subject Usability and Huma Interaction and we worked in a group of 4. We were required to go through a series of question burst exercises to find high level problem that we wanted to solve. After finding the problem we had to research and create a persona and it journey & empathy map. Next we had to come up with and mvp that solved our problem. We also interviewed a few people, which helped us cover major types of users and understand their pain points. We also created a survey on SurveyMonkey to reach more users and get a broader perspective. This helped us identify features which needs to be incorporated and updated in order to create a better user experience.
